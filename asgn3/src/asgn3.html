The Farmer's Dilemma

A farmer must cross a river with a wolf, a goat, and a cabbage.

The boat holds only the farmer and one item.
Left alone: the wolf eats the goat, or the goat eats the cabbage.

Walk through the dock (center of river) to cross.
Press F near an item to pick it up or put it down.
